FAQ

Trust before autonomy.

Can it actually take action?

Yes, but actions are staged behind confirmation gates until the user explicitly approves the exact action, destination, and rationale.

What makes it different from ChatGPT?

JARVIS is designed around persistent memory, specialist agents, scheduled workflows, integrations, auditability, and proactive monitoring.

Is this production-ready?

The current repo is a working demo kernel. The next production step is authenticated FastAPI, persistent storage, OAuth integrations, queues, and observability.
